The world's first cryptocoin invented by a child.

She wanted to trade her made-up money for real money. Her dad said, "No one is going to buy that. Put it on the blockchain." So she did.

First ICO of its kind

Would you like a precious digital piece of the one and only Rainbow Unicoin ever in circulation? Introducing RUC, the digitized version of the original Rainbow Unicoin.

The RUC ICO offers investors a piece of history. Unlike crowdfunding pointless things like potato salad, RUC is a permanent token that you can hold and trade, pointlessly.

I'm intruiged, is this for real?

Yes! RUC is a standard ERC20 token that can be purchased by sending ETH to the RUC contract. The contract sends your ETH to an account created for the little entrepreneur.

Keep in mind, she is not a smart contracts expert. I also asked her for her white paper and she just handed me a blank piece of A4.

How to contribute

Token contract

(this is how you get Rainbow Unicoins)

To participate in the RUC ICO, just send as much Ether as you want to the RUC token/crowdsale contract. You'll get 1000 Rainbow Unicoins for every 1 Ether plus bonus Rainbow Unicoins.

0x1b5ed05690a6b04533f09185afe03ff2371835c2

View on Etherscan or etherchain

The more Ether everyone before you contributes, the more tokens you get.

The token distribution formula is very simple: for every 1 ETH you contribute, you'll receive 1000 RUC. As a bonus you will also receive 500 RUC for every 1 ETH the previous participant contributed, and 250 RUC for every 1 ETH the one before that contributed, and so on. Like this:

Transaction Ether sent Bonus pool RUC received
#1 1.000 ETH 0.000 ETH 1000 RUC
#2 3.000 ETH 5.000 ETH 3500 RUC
#3 0.010 ETH 1.750 ETH 1760 RUC
#4 0.500 ETH 0.880 ETH 1380 RUC
